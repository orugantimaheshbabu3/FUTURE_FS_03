Our Story

More Than Just Coffee —
It's a Feeling

Brewed Awakenings was born from a simple belief: that a great cup of coffee has the power to transform your morning, spark a conversation, or comfort a tired soul.

We source single-origin beans from small farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Vietnam — then roast every batch in-house for peak freshness and flavor.
